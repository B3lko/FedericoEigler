Farm Day

Farm Day is an educational video game that I developed working for Angry Cat Games for the company Legend Of Learning. The game is about solving mini-games and puzzles with the aim of learning concepts about chemistry and physics.

The project was developed entirely with Unity, the scripts are programmed in C# and json files were used to manage the loading of different languages.

Some of the minigames consist of cutting down a tree without the trunks hitting the farmer, placing ingredients in a bowl in a certain order to cook a cake, arranging pipes to make compost and other mechanics

The Endless Dock

The Endless Dock is a 3D Endless Runner, Developed in Unity. It is a video game with a pirate theme where the player must have three lanes and must move between them and jump to avoid the obstacles that approach the dock.

The game was created as the final project of the subject "Manipulation of 3D objects" of the video game design and programming degree.

Both the obstacles and the map are generated as the character reaches the limits of the map.

I programmed a shader that gives the effect of "World Bending" similar to Animal Crossing or Subway Surfer to give the effect that the horizon curves

Santa Fe Runner

Santa Fe Runner is a 2D platform Endless Runner created as a final project for a course. The game was developed using the PIXI.JS library over a period of approximately 3 months. The project automatically adapts to the device it is being played on without the need to configure anything.

The objective is to get the highest score possible by avoiding obstacles that appear randomly. These obstacles can be jumped or the character can roll to go under them. In addition, there are collectible objects that are used to get another chance if you hit an obstacle.
